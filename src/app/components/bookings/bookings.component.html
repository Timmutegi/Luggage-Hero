Bookings :

    Note:

  • You have to check in at the shop you have booked before the end of the day you have booked.
  • You have to adhere to the working hours of the shop.
  • If you fail to do so, your booking will be automatically cancelled.
  • The status of your booking will be "Pending" until you check in at the shop.
  • Once you check in, the status changes to "Active" and when you pay and check out, it will change to "Completed".
